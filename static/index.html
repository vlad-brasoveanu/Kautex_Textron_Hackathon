Upload Planning Sheet

Instantly populate the active scenario by uploading an Excel-exported CSV matrix planning sheet.

Drag and drop your planning CSV here, or click to browse

CSV Layout Format Instructions

Your uploaded CSV must match the standard matrix planning sheet layout:

  • The first row must contain columns: Employee, Team, Location, Hours/Year (or Available hours/year (Region 100%)), Hourly Rate, followed by the specific Topic/Project columns (e.g. Agentic AI, Fuel Project).
  • The rows below should represent employees. The intersection cells represent their allocation percentage (e.g. 20%, 0.2, or 20).
  • Optional additional costs can be appended at the bottom by adding rows starting with cost names (e.g., CAD, Tooling, Prototypes, Recovery) and entering cost amounts under the corresponding topic columns.
